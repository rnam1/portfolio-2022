Forwo

UX / UI / Illustration
Forwo introduces simple ways for young adults to manage their money and start planning their financial future. Simple navigation makes the app easy to use, while playful illustrations and vibrant colours make it an engaging experience.

Hello!

I’m Raha, a Canadian-raised Filipino-Iranian UX/UI designer and illustrator.

I am a wearer of many hats, a multidisciplinary designer and a proud owner of an ever-growing beanie collection. I strive to create thoughtful research-based digital products combined with clever illustrations to elevate the user experience and make people smile.
