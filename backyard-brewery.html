Backyard Brewery is a BC microbrewery that goes out on a limb to craft three highly experimental beer flavours with ingredients that honour the wildness of the West Coast.

Hello!

I’m Raha, a Canadian-raised Filipino-Iranian UX/UI designer and illustrator.

I am a wearer of many hats, a multidisciplinary designer and a proud owner of an ever-growing beanie collection. I strive to create thoughtful research-based digital products combined with clever illustrations to elevate the user experience and bring joy.
