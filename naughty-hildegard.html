Naughty Hildegard

​​​​​​​​​​​​​Branding / Illustration / Packaging / Campaign

This student project is a redesign of an existing beer brand, Driftwood Brewery.

Believed to have been the first to use hops in beer waaaay back in 12th century Germany, Naughty Hildegard is a saucy sister who is more than willing to experiment with very unholy behaviour. Cheers Hildy!
